Our facilities

DAV University campus area is interconnect a variety of campus buildings, including administrative buildings, acedmic buildings, University libraries, campus or student centers, hostel, guest house, gymnasiums, and other outlying structures, like conference centers, technology centers, etc.

Well Maintained Hostel

The university has set up secure hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities including recreation rooms and indoor-game areas. All rooms are equipped with ceiling fans, but the students may opt for air conditioners in their rooms by paying additional charges.
